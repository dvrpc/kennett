Kennett Area
Freight Transportation Study

The Kennett Area Freight Transportation Study is intended to build a better understanding of transportation needs in Kennett Square Borough, Avondale Borough, Kennett Township, East Marlborough Township, London Grove Township, and New Garden Township. This study aims to develop a set of strategies with a comprehensive approach to truck routing and other strategies to meet freight related travel needs in ways that are compatible with other needs in this area.

This web based tool is an effort to better understand the issues in the study area. We are seeking your input on the issues that only you, as a local user of the transportation system or resident of the community can provide. This tool allows you to report issues, concerns, or your own recommendations on the map and provide a brief note to the project team. We will utilize this input to guide our recommendations.
